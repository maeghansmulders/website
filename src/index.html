Hi there,

I’m a product marketer / creative soul that was born in Calgary and lives between Montreal, Canada and San Francisco, California.

I solve problems in unconventional ways: I created ProjectONE12, an initiative to intern at 10 different companies in 112 days across North America. My goal was to experience then define a career path that was right for me.

I love technology like PasswordBox: a startup I joined as Director of Product Marketing. We launched the app from private beta and engaged with millions of users around the world.

I’m grateful for the opportunities with Intel: after PasswordBox was acquired I became an evangelist and strategic awareness specialist. I worked with household names in developing creative campaigns, leading strategic partnership announcements, global training & education. I had the chance to be the face of True Key by Intel Security.
