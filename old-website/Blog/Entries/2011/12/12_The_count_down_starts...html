The count down starts..

Monday, December 12, 2011

 

Oh hey!


So this is when things start to get intense.  :) I have completed 7 out of the 10 jobs for ProjectONE12 and I can hardly believe how quickly time has flown by.  I feel so lucky to have been able to learn so many things, and meet some wonderful, inspiring people along the way.


I woke up this morning and was doing some strategy and planning for the next portion of the project as well as, thinking about what I want to do after.....  and I had three thoughts stick out in my mind in regards to what Project ONE12 has taught me... and they are:






















  1. 1)Curiosity vs. Passion

  2. 2)You are as valuable as your network, not your net-worth

  3. 3)Staying true to who you are


I could go into a lot more detail..... but those thoughts have stuck with me, and been refined as I have grown personally and professional over the last 3 months. 


The reason I only planned 7 jobs in advance was because I was anticipating on having the last 3 internships focused on the industry I want to end up in.  And... exciting news, I have figured that out!!!


I am new, and still learning - but I know that technology is where I want to be.  I love tech because it's the process of taking something and making it better, and has a broad reach on different industries, and skill sets. I really do believe it is the most effective path to channel creativity and make a considerable impact, and that is what I hope to do in the world as I continue to grow as a business women, and life long learner.  :)


Gosh, it has taken a lot to get to this point - asking a ton of questions, working in 7 different jobs, living outside of my comfort zone, dealing with negative criticism, marketing myself, researching, planning as well as, questioning myself to why I want to be in the industry.  But with that said, I am excited to learn more and stoked that the project has given clarity on where I want to be! 


Regardless of what happens next, I know what type of job I am striving for, and that is what I was hoping to achieve :)


Thanks for reading, watching, commenting and sharing.  This adventure is very special to me, and I am so grateful for every day.   My 112th day is January 20th, 2012 - 3 more jobs between now and then, look forward to keeping you posted!


Lots of love,


Just Maeghan
