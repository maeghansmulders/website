About Me

Hi there, I’m Maeghan.

If you don’t know much about me. Let's fix that. After graduating from university, I had no idea what to do with my life. After weeks of stressing, a friend motivated me to create ProjectONE12. A self-created initiative to work at 10 different companies in 112 days across North America in hopes of finding my dream job. After that adventure, I ended up in Montreal, Canada as the Director of Product Marketing for a super rad startup called PasswordBox. Two years later, PasswordBox was acquired by Intel and together we created True Key by Intel Security.

I like to build, learn things and help others. I’m super grateful to have been recognized for my entrepreneurial work, been able to travel broadly, built 6 homes in developing countries and organize women in tech events locally.
